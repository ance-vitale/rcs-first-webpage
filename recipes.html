Spicy chicken & avocado wraps

Pan-fry lean chicken breast with lime, chilli and garlic, then pile onto seeded tortilla wraps. Cool before assembling if packing for lunch.

A simple but delicious lunch or light dinner that packs a punch.

Crusty bread

This all-purpose bread recipe produces a crusty plain white loaf that's classic and delicious. The quantities given in this recipe yield two crusty hearth loaves with an open crumb.

A great starting point for learning to bake bread at home.

Butter ckicken (murgh makhani)

This 20-minute butter chicken is creamy and packed full of flavor. It’s simplified and cooked all in one pan and goes great with a side of rice or naan!

The spicy creaminess is unforgettable and will keep you coming back for more!

Fluffy pancakes

This easy pancake recipe makes light and fluffy pancakes that are great for a weekend brunch. Easily customisable by adding berries, cut-up frit, chocolate-chips etc. to the batter, as well as choosing your own toppings.

You can take this basic recipe and make it your own for a new kind of pancake every weekend.

Mac and Cheese

Mac and Cheese made from scratch is a million times better than a box. This macaroni and cheese is perfectly rich and creamy and it’s sure to be loved by people of all ages! This version is made on the stovetop and uses basic everyday ingredients.

It’s easy enough for weekday dinners but good enough to serve at holiday get togethers.
