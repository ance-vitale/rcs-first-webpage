Spicy chicken & avocado wraps

Instructions
  • 1 chicken breast
  • juice of ½ lime
  • ½ tsp mild chilli powder
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 2 seeded tortilla wraps
  • 1 avocado, halved and stoned
  • 1 canned roasted red capsicum in oil, sliced
  • fresh coriander chopped
Ingredients
  1. Thinly slice the chicken at an angle so you have quite large pieces of chicken, then mix with the lime juice, chilli powder and garlic.
  2. Heat the oil in a non-stick frying pan, then fry the chicken for a couple of mins – it will cook very quickly so keep an eye on it – while you warm the wraps following the pack instructions.
  3. Squash half an avocado onto each wrap, add the roasted capsicum to the pan to warm them through, then pile onto the wraps, and sprinkle over the coriander. Roll up, cut in half and serve.
